1. make an offsec.com account
	1. pay $15/mo for their "labs access", don't buy the expensive ones
2. use LainKusanagi's OSCP list on reddit
  1. https://www.reddit.com/r/oscp/comments/1c8pzyz/lainkusanagi_list_of_oscp_like_machines/ 
3. search for "<BOXNAME> OSCP" if stuck, use medium.com guides
4. generally,
  a. nmap target
  b. read nmap output
  c. spider websites with dirb
  d. try easy to guess credentials
  e. use hacktricks.xyz
  f. use linPEAS or winPEAS
  g. use Claude Code when stuck
5. when you find an exploit,
  a. use `searchsploit TECHNOLOGY_NAME` to search for exploits
6. when really stuck,
  a. Visit https://github.com/meltingscales/oscp/tree/main/boxes/provinggrounds
  b. look for other OSCP writeups on medium.com or youtube
7. HackTheBox is harder, but a great resource
  a. They have a "guided mode" that is great for learning
  b. lots of medium articles are written about HackTheBox, too
